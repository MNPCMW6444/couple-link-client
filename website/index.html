The Science of Love

Cognitive Behavioral Couples Therapy (CBCT) is a an evidence approach in relationship therapy that centers on the idea that our thoughts, emotions, and behaviors are deeply interconnected and significantly influence our romantic relationships. By adopting the principles of CBCT, our AI-enhanced therapy tool offers couples a unique avenue to explore and transform their relational dynamics.

Integrative Approach
